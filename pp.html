Bansly Bot - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Bansly ("Bot", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Discord bot. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User Information

  • User IDs: When you use moderation commands, we process Discord user IDs to execute requested actions.
  • Usernames: We process Discord usernames in conjunction with user IDs for moderation purposes.
  • Moderation reasons: When provided during moderation actions, we process the reasons you provide.

1.2 Server Information

  • Server IDs: We process server IDs to perform moderation actions across multiple servers.
  • Server names: We process server names to help identify where actions are being performed.
  • Ban lists: When exported, server ban lists are temporarily processed by the Bot.

1.3 Command Usage

Command invocation data: We log when moderation commands are used, including who used them, in which server, and when they were used.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To execute moderation actions as requested (kick, ban, unban)
  • To provide multi-server moderation capabilities
  • To generate exportable ban lists
  • To process imported ban lists
  • To maintain logs of bot usage for operational purposes
  • To prevent abuse of the Bot's features

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Bot Logs

The Bot maintains logs of command usage for operational and security purposes. These logs include:

  • The Discord username and ID of the user who executed a command
  • The server name and ID where the command was executed
  • The type of command that was executed
  • Timestamps of when commands were executed

3.2 Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The information is only accessible to the Bot developers and is used solely for operating and improving the Bot.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the personal information we collect. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

5.1 Command Logs

Logs of command usage are retained for operational purposes and may be deleted periodically.

5.2 Ban Lists

  • Exported ban lists: When you export ban lists, this data is temporarily processed and then delivered to you. We keep a copy of the exported lists to confirm functionality.
  • Imported ban lists: When you import ban lists, we process this data to execute the requested bans. We do not retain the imported lists after processing.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about what data we have processed related to your Discord account
  • Request deletion of any logs specifically identifying you (where feasible)
  • Opt out of having your server data processed by removing the Bot from your server

7. Discord's Privacy Policy

As our Bot operates through Discord, information we process is also subject to Discord's Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing Discord's Privacy Policy as well.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Bot owner through Discord in the Support Server.

By using Bansly, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
